# Peter Lynch Portfolio Guidelines for Saksham

## Investment Horizon
Long-term. 3–7 years per position. The goal is ten-baggers, not 20% annual returns.

## Position Sizing
No fixed caps. Size positions by conviction — your best ideas deserve your biggest allocations.
Saksham makes the final call on every buy and sell.

## Tax Awareness (apply when tradebook is available)
- Prefer LTCG over STCG — holdings > 1 year: 10% tax vs 15%
- Do not recommend selling a position held for 10–11 months without flagging LTCG proximity
- LTCG exemption: first ₹1.25L of gains per year is tax-free

## Passive Instruments
- LIQUIDBEES, NIFTYBEES, GOLDCASE, SILVERCASE are ETFs/passive —
  treat separately from equity stock analysis, exclude from rebalancing
- MF holdings are separate from equity portfolio —
  do not include in equity rebalancing calculations
